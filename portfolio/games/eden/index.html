Eden

Eden is currently in the prototype stage hence there is no download.

Sneak your way through dangerous situations and unravel a mystery that surrounds the beginning of your home town of Eden. Use the artefacts you find on your way to discover its dark past and the depths of its corruption.

During gameplay you will be exploring the world in 1st person, using the mouse to interact with plants making them shrink or grow.

Moodboard

With this game i want to go for a dark mysterious but cartoony style within a steampunk setting.

The Concept

Eden is a game about controlling nature to help explore the towers of a city called Eden. you play as a child who discovers gauntlets which allow you to control the growth and decay of plants.

The Prototypes

So for this concept I have currently made two small prototypes for the plant growing concept.

The two prototypes do the same thing but in two different ways.
The first one changes the shrinkwrap value of a 3d Model to be equal to the current amount of souls divided by the maximum amount of souls.
The second prototype gets a value in the same way but instead it plays an animation until it reaches said value at which point it pauses, and to make it shrink, it just plays the animation in reverse till it reaches its new value.

I feel like the second option is more versitile as I can make the animated object move in any way I wish using a bone rig. hence ill be moving forward with this technique.

Bonus!

I was working on several designs for the souls, my citeria is that they would look atherial and would be easy to model in low poly, as I would like this game to be able to run on any hardware, as such I came up with a design I am very happy with.
